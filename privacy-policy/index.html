Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cardone Capital, LLC ("Cardone Capital," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you visit cardonecapital.com or interact with our investor relations team.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated automatically by your use of our website.

Information You Provide

When you submit a contact form, request a free book, book an investor call, or communicate with our team, we collect:

  • Name — first and last name as entered in forms
  • Email address — used for all follow-up communications
  • Phone number — when provided, used for investor relations calls
  • Accredited investor status — as represented in form submissions
  • Fund interest — which fund(s) you indicate interest in (Fund 29, Fund 30, IRA/401k)
  • Message content — any information you include in the contact form message field

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we and our third-party analytics and tracking partners automatically collect:

  • Pages visited, time on page, navigation path, and scroll depth
  • Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution
  • IP address (anonymized for analytics purposes)
  • Referral source — how you arrived at our website
  • Session recordings and heatmaps (via Microsoft Clarity — no personally identifiable information captured)
  • Cookie identifiers (see Cookies section below)

How We Use Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your information is used exclusively to communicate with you about investment opportunities at Cardone Capital and to improve our website.

Specifically, we use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to investor inquiries and conduct investor relations calls
  • Manage leads through our HubSpot CRM and assign them to the appropriate internal IR team member
  • Deliver free books and educational materials via email (HubSpot sequences #1–5, one per book requested)
  • Send follow-up nurture emails related to the book or resource you downloaded — you may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email
  • Analyze site usage to improve content, navigation, and conversion
  • Verify accredited investor status prior to providing fund subscription documents

Third-Party Data Processors

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and investor relations function. Each processes your data under its own privacy policy and terms of service.

HS

HubSpot, Inc.

All form submissions, calendar bookings, and book download requests are processed by HubSpot and stored in our HubSpot CRM. HubSpot also powers our email follow-up sequences. Data is stored on HubSpot's servers (US or EU, depending on account configuration). HubSpot Privacy Policy ↗

GA4

Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. IP addresses are anonymized. GA4 uses cookies to distinguish visitors and track sessions. Data is retained per your GA4 retention settings (default 14 months). You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on ↗. Google Privacy Policy ↗

MS

Microsoft Clarity (Microsoft Corporation)

We use Microsoft Clarity for session recording and heatmap analytics. Clarity does not capture personally identifiable information (PII) — it observes anonymized mouse movements, clicks, and scroll behavior. No form field content is recorded. Microsoft Privacy Statement ↗

AF

AppFolio, Inc.

Existing investors access their accounts through the AppFolio investor portal (investors.appfolioim.com/cardonecapital/investor/login). Data within the AppFolio portal — investment balances, distribution history, tax documents — is processed by AppFolio under a separate data processing agreement. AppFolio's privacy practices govern data within the investor portal.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to enable site functionality and analytics. The cookies placed on your device include:

  • Google Analytics cookies — _ga, _ga_XXXXXXXX — session and traffic analytics, 2-year retention
  • HubSpot tracking cookie — _hsq, hubspotutk — contact identification after form fill, 13-month retention
  • Microsoft Clarity cookie — _clsk, _clck — session recording, 1-year retention

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and forms. For instructions, visit your browser's help documentation (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Cardone Capital does not sell personal information. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to Know. You may request a copy of the personal information we have collected about you, the categories of information collected, and the purposes for which it was used.
  • Right to Delete. You may request deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale. We do not sell personal information. No action is required to opt out.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at invest@cardonecapital.com with the subject line "CCPA Privacy Request." We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to maintain an investor relationship or as required by applicable law:

  • HubSpot CRM data — retained per HubSpot's data retention policy while your contact record is active. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Google Analytics data — retained for 14 months per default GA4 retention settings.
  • Microsoft Clarity — session recordings retained for 90 days per Clarity's default policy.
  • Email unsubscribe — if you unsubscribe from our emails, we retain your email address on a suppression list to ensure you are not re-added inadvertently.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All data transmitted to our forms is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. HubSpot, Google, and Microsoft maintain their own security certifications for data stored on their platforms.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
