Eligibility Requirement

All Cardone Capital fund investments are offered exclusively to accredited investors under SEC Regulation D, Rule 506(c). You qualify as an accredited investor if you meet at least one of the following:

  • Net worth test: Net worth exceeding $1 million (individually or jointly with spouse), excluding the value of your primary residence.
  • Income test (individual): Annual income exceeding $200,000 in each of the past two years, with reasonable expectation of the same this year.
  • Income test (joint): Joint income with spouse exceeding $300,000 in each of the past two years, with reasonable expectation of the same this year.
  • Professional certification: Holding a FINRA Series 7, 65, or 82 license in good standing — regardless of income or net worth.
  • Knowledgeable employee: A "knowledgeable employee" of the fund as defined under Investment Advisers Act.

Not sure if you qualify? Schedule a call with our team — we'll walk you through the eligibility requirements confidentially and with no obligation to invest. Book a Free Call →

Accredited investor status is self-certified at the time of investment and verified during the subscription process per SEC Rule 506(c) requirements. This is not legal advice. Consult your own counsel regarding your specific eligibility.
