The Investment Thesis

Real Estate Funds Bitcoin.

Most investors try to time Bitcoin. Fund 29 does it differently — the real estate earns the Bitcoin. Systematic, disciplined, backed by hard assets.

01

Acquire Institutional Real Estate

Cardone Capital purchases Class-A institutional multifamily properties — 46 assets, ~14,600 units, across FL, TX, AZ, GA, AL, and MD. Properties selected for strong cash flow characteristics, appreciation potential, and market fundamentals.

02

Generate Stable Cash Flow

Tenants pay rent. Properties generate income. That cash flow is distributed quarterly to investors — and a systematic portion is earmarked for the Bitcoin accumulation strategy. The real estate funds the Bitcoin.

03

Accumulate Bitcoin

The Bitcoin accumulation strategy runs systematically in the background. No market timing. No speculation. Real estate income methodically converts to Bitcoin exposure — giving investors the asymmetric digital upside without gambling directly on price.

Eligibility Requirement

Fund 29 is offered exclusively to accredited investors under SEC Regulation D, Rule 506(c). You qualify if you meet at least one of the following criteria:

  • Net worth over $1M — individually or jointly with your spouse, excluding the value of your primary residence.
  • Income over $200,000/year (individual) — in each of the past two years, with expectation of the same this year.
  • Joint income over $300,000/year — with your spouse, in each of the past two years, with expectation of the same this year.
  • FINRA Series 7, 65, or 82 license — in good standing, regardless of income or net worth.
