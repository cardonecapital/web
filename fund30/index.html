Distribution Mechanics

How You Get Paid Every Quarter

Four times a year, real estate income converts into cash in your account. Here's the exact mechanism.

01

Tenants Pay Rent

~14,600 units across 46 Class-A properties collect rental income every month. High-quality tenants in strong Sun Belt and growth markets. Professional third-party management keeps vacancy low and rent collection consistent.

02

Deduct Operating Costs

From gross rental income, the fund covers property operating expenses, debt service, management fees, and reserves for capital expenditures. Cardone Capital's operating discipline — refined over 35+ years — keeps expense ratios controlled and distributable income maximized.

03

Cash Distributed Quarterly

Remaining cash flow after expenses is distributed directly to Fund 30 investors four times per year: April (Q1), July (Q2), October (Q3), and January (Q4). Cash goes straight to your bank account or IRA custodian. No Bitcoin. No complexity. Just income.

Eligibility Requirement

Fund 30 is offered exclusively to accredited investors per SEC Regulation D, Rule 506(c). You qualify if you meet at least one of:

  • Net worth over $1M — individually or jointly with spouse, excluding your primary residence.
  • Income over $200,000/year (individual) — in each of the past two years, with expectation of the same this year.
  • Joint income over $300,000/year — with your spouse, in each of the past two years with expectation of the same.
  • FINRA Series 7, 65, or 82 license — in good standing, regardless of income or net worth.

Not sure if you qualify? Our team will walk you through it confidentially with no obligation. Book a Free Call →
