Before Your Call

What to Expect on
Your Investor Call

1

We'll review your investment objectives

Our investor relations specialist will ask about your goals, timeline, and current portfolio. This is not a sales pitch — it's a structured conversation to understand whether Fund 29, Fund 30, or an IRA structure is the right fit for your situation. You're encouraged to ask every question you have.

2

We'll walk you through the fund mechanics

You'll get a clear explanation of how your chosen fund works — how capital is deployed, how distributions are paid, what the minimum investment is, and what the subscription process looks like from start to first distribution. No jargon. No pressure. Just clarity.

3

You'll leave knowing your next step

By the end of the call, you'll know exactly whether Cardone Capital is right for you — and if so, what the next step is. You're under zero obligation to invest. The call exists to give you the information you need to make a confident decision.

💡

Quick prep tip

Have a rough sense of how much capital you'd like to deploy and whether you're investing personally, through an LLC, or through a Self-Directed IRA. The call goes faster and you'll get more out of it.
