§ 01 / Overview

Who We Are & How We Operate

Cardone Capital is a vertically integrated real estate investment firm — we acquire, manage, and operate institutional multifamily real estate and offer accredited investors direct participation through private fund structures.

"To make institutional real estate accessible to accredited investors — delivering the same returns previously reserved for large institutions, at the individual investor level."

— Cardone Capital Mission

Before Cardone Capital, institutional-quality multifamily real estate was accessible only to pension funds, endowments, and ultra-high-net-worth family offices. Minimum investments started in the millions. Individual investors were locked out entirely.

Since 2016, Cardone Capital has allowed accredited investors to co-invest directly alongside Grant Cardone in the same institutional assets — same Class-A properties, same professional management, same quarterly distributions.
